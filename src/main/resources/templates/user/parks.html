Parks in Rwanda


Akagera National park

Location:
Entrance Fee: 30,000 Rwf
Description:

Akagera National Park is located in eastern Rwanda along the international border with Tanzania.

It covers 1,122 km² (433 sq mi) and includes savannah, montane and swamp habitats.

The park is named for the Kagera River which flows along its eastern boundary feeding into Lake Ihema and several smaller lakes.

Working Days: Monday - Saturday
Working Hours: 6AM - 6PM



Nyungwe National park

Location: KN6, Rusizi, Rwanda
Entrance Fee: 25,000 Rwf for kids, 35,000Rwf for Adults
Description:

One of the oldest rainforests in Africa, Nyungwe is rich in biodiversity and spectacularly beautiful.

The mountainous region is teaming with wildlife, including a small population of chimpanzees as well as 12 other species of primate,

including the L’Hoest’s monkey endemic to the Albertine Rift.

Working Days: Monday - Saturday
Working Hours: 9AM - 5PM



Volcanoes National park

Location:
Entrance Fee: 50,000 Rwf
Description:

Situated in the far northwest of Rwanda, Volcanoes National Park protects the steep slopes of this magnificent mountain range – home

of the endangered mountain gorilla and a rich mosaic of montane ecosystems,

which embrace evergreen and bamboo forest, open grassland, swamp and heath.

Working Days: Monday - Saturday
Working Hours: 9AM - 6PM

For more information visit the official Visit Rwanda website.
